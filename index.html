About Us

A Legacy of Learning
& Excellence Since 2016

Founded in 2016, Emalashira Primary School has grown into one of the most respected schools in Nairobi — known for academic rigour, warm pastoral care and a joyful learning culture.

We follow Kenya's Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) with a firm belief that every child is unique, capable and worthy of the very best. Our approach blends rigorous academics with creativity, values and life skills.

Why Emalashira?

Built for Every Child
to Thrive

We go beyond the classroom to develop well-rounded, confident and compassionate individuals ready for tomorrow's world.

CBC Curriculum

Fully aligned to Kenya's Competency-Based Curriculum with strong emphasis on practical skills, critical thinking and character.

Small Class Sizes

Average class size of 28 pupils ensures every child receives personalised attention and no voice goes unheard in the classroom.

Safe & Caring

Secure gated compound, CCTV, pastoral care team, regular health checks and a comprehensive child protection policy.

Holistic Growth

Music, arts, PE, scouting, debate, coding club, football, volleyball, swimming, drama and much more.

Expert Teachers

Qualified educators continuously trained in modern CBC pedagogy, child psychology and digital teaching tools.

Parent Portal

Real-time access to attendance, grades, fees and direct teacher communication — 24/7 on any device.

Modern Facilities

Equipped science lab, computer lab, library, multipurpose hall, sports fields and a clean well-maintained campus.

Nutrition Programme

Balanced hot lunch served daily, dietitian-approved menu and a school garden where pupils grow their own food.
